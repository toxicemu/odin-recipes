Grilled Cheese

Description

A nice warm and buttery cheese sandwich that's great for a cold day!

Ingredients

  • 4 slices white bread
  • 3 tablespoons butter, divided
  • 2 slices Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Gather all the ingredients.
  2. Preheat a nonstick skillet over medium heat.
  3. Generously butter one sie of a slice of bread.
  4. Place bread butter-side down in the hot skillet; add 1 slice of cheese.
  5. Butter a second slice of bread on one side and place butter-side up on top of cheese.
  6. Cook untill lightly browned on one side; flip over and continue to cook until cheese is melted.
  7. Repeat process with remaining ingredients, plate and enjoy!
