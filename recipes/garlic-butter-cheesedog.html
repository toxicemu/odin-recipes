Garlic Butter Cheesedog

Description

A wonderfully decadent comfort food for any time of the year. Perfect to dazzle with minimalist ingredients!

Ingredients

  • Four hot dogs
  • One fourth cup garlic butter, divided
  • Four hot dog buns
  • One cup shredded cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Score hot dogs, brush with garlic butter, and place on a baking sheet.
  2. Bake hot dogs in the preheated oven until nicely browned, 6 to 8 minutes.
  3. Meanwhile, brush insides of hot dog buns with garlic butter; toast buns in a large skillet over medium heat until golden brown. Set aside.
  4. In the same skillet, lay down 1/4 cup cheese and place a bun face down on top of cheese. Cook undisturbed until cheese is melted. Remove from skillet and repeat with remaining buns.
  5. Place hot dogs in prepared buns and serve immediately.
